About Me

The Creative Journey

I'm an indie game developer actively building 2D and 3D games in Unity using C#. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science at the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

Through personal projects, I've explored physics-based mechanics, UI/UX design, animation, and level design. My work includes titles like Beat Breaker and Coin Collector, reflecting a strong foundation in Unity development, creativity, and a self-driven approach to learning.

As a Game Developer Team Member at Google Developer Groups on Campus - UMT, I contribute to designing and developing games, working on gameplay mechanics and interactive systems while supporting team members through workshops and collaborative development sessions.
